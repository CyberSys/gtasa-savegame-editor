0123 4567 89AB CDEF 0123 4567 89AB CDEF
0000 XYZ F1F2F3 F4F5F6 Type ModModMod ModModMod
0020 ModModMod ModModMod ModModMod C1C2 RS 00 PJ Ni XYZ 00
  • X, Y and Z are the position of the vehicle
  • RS is the radio station:
    • 00 = Vehicle has no radio
    • 01 = Playback FM
    • 02 = K Rose
    • 03 = K-DST
    • 04 = Bounce FM
    • 05 = SF-UR
    • 06 = Radio Los Santos
    • 07 = Radio X
    • 08 = CSR 103.9
    • 09 = K-Jah West
    • 0A = Master Sounds 98.3
    • 0B = WCTR
    • 0C = User Track Player
    • 0D = Radio Off
  • PJ is the paint job.
  • Ni is the number of nitrous left.
  • The last X, Y and Z indicate the angle. X and Y can't be 00 both.
    Keep in mind that these are signed values.
    Some examples:
    • 00 01 00 = North
    • FF 00 00 = West
    • 00 FF 00 = South
    • 01 00 00 = East
    • 00 01 03 = North, 72° Up
    • 00 01 FD = North, 72° Down
